Reversing the Gaze: Amar Singh's diary - Amar Singh's life has crossed paths with some of the more interesting and underrated parts of modern Indian History(The chapter on the Boxer Rebellion and days in the Imperial serice corp are my favourite). Military history enthusiasts would love his accounts. Goldmine as a primary source from an Indian perspective.
The complete works of Swami Vivekananda - My introduction to Indian Philosophy in general and Shankara's Vedanta in particular. Do not expect a rigourous and complete explanation for Vedanta. At best an initiation. A very good initiation.
